Project Aristotle

Project Aristotle aims to unite people to stand up against cyber bullying by providing them with an interface to oppose negative comments! We took on this project at Hack Princeton and won the sponsor prize from Indico for best use of their sentiment api. It was inspired by Monica Lewinsky and her recent TED talk against cyber bullying.     The Lazy Car

    The "Lazy Car" was a RC car that was modded to allow users to drive using only hand gestures with the Leap Motion and arduinos to control the circuits. There was also a camera attached to the car that would allow users to get a "life like" driving experience using the Oculus VR headset. This was our entry at Hack Western 2015.

    Automated Defense Turret

    The Auto Defense Turret is a lego NXT controlled nerf turret. Drive this beast into your dorm room hallway, activate its defense mode and no will be able to get past its flying nerf darts of destruction! With target lock on capabilities and a range of up to 30ft your entire apartment will be safe from intruders.. until the batteries die.
